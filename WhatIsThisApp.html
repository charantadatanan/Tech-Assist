TECHASSIST

What is TechAssist?

TechAssist is a simple and user-friendly application designed to help senior citizens understand and use modern technology. It provides easy-to-follow guides, helpful tools, and instant support to make everyday digital tasks easier.

What Can It Do?

Phone Assistance

Learn how to use smartphones, apps, and basic settings step by step.

Email Setup

Guides for creating, sending, and managing emails safely.

Safety Tips

Protect yourself from scams, phishing, and online threats.

Auto Chat Help

Get instant answers and assistance anytime through the built-in chat feature.

Our Mission

TechAssist aims to bridge the digital gap by empowering seniors with the confidence and skills needed to use technology independently and safely.
