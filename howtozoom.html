Accessibility Guide

Learn how to adjust the view of this website.

Keyboard Shortcuts

You can use your browser's built-in zoom features to make text larger or smaller:

  • Zoom In: Press Ctrl + + (Windows) or Cmd + + (Mac)
  • Zoom Out: Press Ctrl + - (Windows) or Cmd + - (Mac)
  • Reset to 100%: Press Ctrl + 0 (Windows) or Cmd + 0 (Mac)

Mouse Controls

Hold the Ctrl or Cmd key and scroll your mouse wheel up or down to zoom.
